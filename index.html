Only use E85 Flex Fuel if your car is prepared!

E85 Flex Fuel is a blend of 85% ethanol and 15% gas that CANNOT be used in every engine! Make sure you have a FlexFuel car before even thinking about purchasing E85. Many manufacturers use yellow gas caps to signify that E85 is permitted, but this may be different by country/region/etc.
